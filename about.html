About Calorie Tracker

Calorie Tracker is a simple and easy-to-use application that helps you keep track of your daily calorie intake. Our mission is to empower you to make informed decisions about your health and well-being.

This application is designed to:

  • Log your food intake and calories.
  • Track your progress over time.
  • Help you stay motivated on your journey to a healthier lifestyle.

We believe that everyone deserves access to tools that can help them live a healthier and happier life.

FAQ

  • Q: What is a calorie?

    A: A calorie is a unit of energy. When you eat food, your body breaks it down and uses the calories for energy.

  • Q: How many calories should I eat per day?

    A: The number of calories you should eat per day depends on your age, gender, activity level, and other factors. Consult a doctor or registered dietitian for personalized advice.

  • Q: What are some healthy foods to eat?

    A: Some healthy foods to eat include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and low-fat dairy products.

  • Q: What are some tips for losing weight?

    A: Some tips for losing weight include eating a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep.

  • Q: How can I use this app to track my calorie intake?

    A: Simply enter the name of the food you ate and the number of calories it contains. The app will keep track of your total calorie intake.
